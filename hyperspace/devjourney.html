Intention

I developed this demo to demo-n-strate my Unreal capabilities. While I have ensured the basic features of a simple 3rd-person melee combat game is to be executed well, I brought on more focus to sequences and animation, as I believed cinematics is my thing and is fun to do.

This project was actually started in Unreal Enigne 4. When I picked it up again in Oct 2022, I successfully converted it to Unreal Engine 5.0

Why Aurora

I came across this Hero character in the UE Marketplace and know for certain I would want to showcase my Unreal capabilities with her. Also, I like all things Aurora, the magical waves in the sky of Iceland, the beloved Norwegian singer, sleeping...

The final product from level creation. The images above is from God of War (2018), showing the Niflheim brigde.

Level Inspiration

The level was created with assets from the Paragon Props pack in the UE Marketplace. It is heavily inspired by the Bridge in Niflheim from God of War (2018) and its sequel.
